Purpose

Mr. President is an interactive timeline of the United States presidents. Each president photo on the timeline is linked to a profile page with fun facts, historical and biographical information, and a ranking feature. The ranking featureallows you to rank each president on a scale of 0-100. On the ranking page, you can see the weekly trends of how each president scores up. The page allows the user to view historical trends of how their favorite president falls in the rankings from week to week! We hope you enjoy learning about the American Presidents!
